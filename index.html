PBT Keycap Lookbook

A hand-verified shortlist of PBT keycap sets in the flat & uniform profile family (DSA / SA / XDA / MOA / MDA / KAT), sorted into three aesthetics — sourced from Etsy and specialist keycap retailers. Every listing below was opened and confirmed live.

How this list was built

  • Material: PBT only — doubleshot or dye-sublimation. Anything ABS (or unstated) was dropped.
  • Profile: the flat / uniform / tall-sculpted family only — DSA, SA, XDA, MOA, MDA, KAT. Standard Cherry/OEM sculpts were excluded from the main picks (a few exceptional ones appear in the labelled “near-miss” rows). Profile is called out on every card.
  • Where from: Etsy plus specialist retailers (PopKeyCap, KeyArtisan, KeyCapUS, prototypist, Keysium…) where they had the better set. Each buy button says where that one lives.
  • Design: genuinely on-theme for its bucket — no generic “Japanese” or “gamer RGB” filler.
  • Live check: each listing was opened in a browser; dead, sold-out and “item unavailable” listings were removed.
Prices are in USD and were captured on 4 July 2026; most listings were on a limited-time sale, so the number you see on Etsy may differ. Etsy listings expire, sell out, and change variants without notice — if a link is dead by the time you read this, search the shop name. Links are canonical etsy.com/listing/… URLs with no affiliate wrappers or trackers. Keycaps only — boards and switches shown in photos are not included.
Bucket 01

Cyberpunk · Bi-Lighting & Neon-Noir

The 2018 “bisexual lighting” wash — soft-but-saturated pink and magenta into purple into electric blue, romantic neon dusk (Atomic Blonde, San Junipero, John Wick). The catch: that exact pink/purple/blue palette lives almost entirely in Cherry-profile sets, which the flat-profile rule excludes. So the main grid holds the closest flat-profile picks — a charcoal/violet XDA, a violet/cyan MDA, a warm Blade-Runner-orange gradient — and the near-miss row below is where the pure bi-lighting sets live. Start with Neon Abyss.

Bucket 02

Pastel · Mintlodify · Cottagecore-Witch

Soft sage, matcha, lavender and butter-yellow; gentle gradients and botanical / celestial motifs. The deep bucket — led by YuniKeyCo, whose clean gradient-minimalist MOA sets (the lavender you loved, plus its rainbow, sakura and ocean siblings) anchor it, alongside the simple two-tone matcha and a zodiac constellation board.

Bucket 03

Ukiyo-e Heroes · Game-Art & Woodblock

Named for Jed Henry’s series, this bucket is about interesting artistic takes on the same IPs he reimagines — Mario, Pokémon, Kirby, Studio Ghibli — plus the woodblock elements that inspired them. So it runs from a pixel-art Mario and a pink Kirby gradient to a genuine aizuri-e Great Wave and painterly sumi-e landscapes. The art and the character are the point; flat / uniform profile and PBT throughout.

YuniKeyCo is the star of the pastel bucket — clean gradient-minimalist and soft-illustrative MOA sets (lavender, rainbow, sakura, ocean). PixelKeys is the deepest Etsy catalogue of XDA/MOA PBT across every theme. For game-IP art, PopKeyCap designs restrained sets (the Mario), KeyArtisan does cute illustrated ones with flat-profile options (the Kirby), and KeycapsCustom covers Ghibli / Zelda. Off Etsy for the neon: Keysium has a deep bi-lighting catalogue (Neon Abyss, Kabukicho, Retrowave), KeyCapUS is the cheap flat-profile XDA source, and prototypist resells Keyreative’s KAT Great Wave.

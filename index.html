PBT Keycap Lookbook

A hand-verified shortlist of PBT keycap sets in the flat & uniform profile family (DSA / SA / XDA / MOA / MDA / KAT), sorted into three aesthetics — sourced from Etsy and specialist keycap retailers. Every listing below was opened and confirmed live.

◤ Cyberpunk / Synthwave ✿ Pastel / Witch 波 Ukiyo-e

How this list was built

  • Material: PBT only — doubleshot or dye-sublimation. Anything ABS (or unstated) was dropped.
  • Profile: the flat / uniform / tall-sculpted family only — DSA, SA, XDA, MOA, MDA, KAT. Standard Cherry/OEM sculpts were excluded from the main picks (a few exceptional ones appear in the labelled “near-miss” rows). Profile is called out on every card.
  • Where from: mostly Etsy, plus a few from specialist retailers (KeyCapUS, prototypist) where Etsy came up short. The button says where each one lives.
  • Design: genuinely on-theme for its bucket — no generic “Japanese” or “gamer RGB” filler.
  • Live check: each listing was opened in a browser; dead, sold-out and “item unavailable” listings were removed.
Prices are in USD and were captured on 4 July 2026; most listings were on a limited-time sale, so the number you see on Etsy may differ. Etsy listings expire, sell out, and change variants without notice — if a link is dead by the time you read this, search the shop name. Links are canonical etsy.com/listing/… URLs with no affiliate wrappers or trackers. Keycaps only — boards and switches shown in photos are not included.
Bucket 01

Cyberpunk · Synthwave · Blade Runner

Neon-on-dark, hot magenta and electric cyan, orange-on-teal dystopia, outrun gradients. The hard truth of this bucket: tasteful neon-synthwave in a flat profile barely exists in PBT — the look lives almost entirely in Cherry-profile GMK-clones, which the rule excludes. What survives is small but honest: a Blade-Runner-orange gradient, a neon-purple/cyan set, a translucent-black SA set, and — going beyond Etsy — a genuinely good charcoal/violet XDA set from KeyCapUS. Two Cherry/backlit near-misses are shown below too.

Near misses — right vibe, outside the flat-profile spec

These nail the neon-noir look but are Cherry profile or have an unlisted/backlit profile, so they sit outside the strict rule. Shown for completeness.

Bucket 02

Pastel · Mintlodify · Cottagecore-Witch

Soft sage, matcha, lavender and butter-yellow; botanical and celestial motifs — herbs, moons, stars, constellations. This is the deep bucket: uniform XDA and MOA sets in gentle, low-contrast palettes that sit right next to the mintlodify / casidoo world. From a literal cottagecore-witch herb set to a zodiac constellation board.

Near miss — right vibe, wrong profile

Lush and on-theme, but Cherry profile.

Bucket 03

Ukiyo-e · Japanese Woodblock

Genuine woodblock-print aesthetic — Hokusai waves, Mount Fuji, cranes and junks in indigo, ochre, vermilion and off-white (the Jed Henry / Ukiyo-e Heroes palette). Not “anything Japanese”: cute-Pokémon and anime sets were rejected. The catch mirrors cyberpunk — most true woodblock sets are Cherry/OEM; the flat-profile survivors are a tight MOA cluster on Etsy, crowned by the off-Etsy KAT Great Wave (aizuri-e indigo, the gold standard). All beautiful.

Near misses — worth knowing about

The iconic Great Wave set (Cherry + RGB shine-through) and a spec-perfect MOA Pokémon set whose art is cute sprites rather than woodblock.

Shops worth watching

PixelKeys Capsora YuniKeyCo KeyArtisann AdventureKeycaps KeysCreationns AstroKaps NoisyCaps KeyCapUS ↗ prototypist ↗

PixelKeys is the single best Etsy source — a huge, consistently-live catalogue of XDA and MOA PBT sets across every theme (it shows up in all three buckets). Capsora and YuniKeyCo are the go-to for MOA Japanese-woodblock and pastel-gradient sets; KeyArtisann / KeyArtisanUS carry the widest XDA/MDA themed range (though many are Cherry-only — check the profile before buying). AdventureKeycaps made the celestial constellation set, and KeysCreationns the painterly woodblock landscape. Off Etsy, KeyCapUS is a cheap source of flat-profile XDA dye-sub, and prototypist (reselling Keyreative’s KAT sets) is where the Great Wave lives.

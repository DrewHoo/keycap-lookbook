PBT Keycap Lookbook

A hand-verified shortlist of PBT keycap sets in the flat & uniform profile family (DSA / SA / XDA / MOA / MDA / KAT), sorted into three aesthetics — sourced from Etsy and specialist keycap retailers. Every listing below was opened and confirmed live.

◤ Cyberpunk / Bi-Lighting ✿ Pastel / Witch 波 Ukiyo-e Heroes

How this list was built

  • Material: PBT only — doubleshot or dye-sublimation. Anything ABS (or unstated) was dropped.
  • Profile: the flat / uniform / tall-sculpted family only — DSA, SA, XDA, MOA, MDA, KAT. Standard Cherry/OEM sculpts were excluded from the main picks (a few exceptional ones appear in the labelled “near-miss” rows). Profile is called out on every card.
  • Where from: Etsy plus specialist retailers (PopKeyCap, KeyArtisan, KeyCapUS, prototypist, Keysium…) where they had the better set. Each buy button says where that one lives.
  • Design: genuinely on-theme for its bucket — no generic “Japanese” or “gamer RGB” filler.
  • Live check: each listing was opened in a browser; dead, sold-out and “item unavailable” listings were removed.
Prices are in USD and were captured on 4 July 2026; most listings were on a limited-time sale, so the number you see on Etsy may differ. Etsy listings expire, sell out, and change variants without notice — if a link is dead by the time you read this, search the shop name. Links are canonical etsy.com/listing/… URLs with no affiliate wrappers or trackers. Keycaps only — boards and switches shown in photos are not included.
Bucket 01

Cyberpunk · Bi-Lighting & Neon-Noir

The 2018 “bisexual lighting” wash — soft-but-saturated pink and magenta into purple into electric blue, romantic neon dusk (Atomic Blonde, San Junipero, John Wick). Honest finding after going wide: that exact pink/purple/blue palette lives almost entirely in Cherry-profile sets, so genuinely flat-profile and in-stock bi-lighting is scarce — this is the hardest bucket to fill. The grid holds the real flat-profile finds — a pink→violet→blue “Starry Sky”, a charcoal neon-noir, clean violet gradients, cold-blue techno — and the expanded near-miss row below is where the pure-palette sets live, including two that finally get the profile right (uniform KAM) but are between production runs.

Near misses — right vibe, outside the flat-profile spec

Two kinds of near-miss. First, the pure pink/purple/blue palette in Cherry (sculpted) profile — the truest match to the vibe if you’ll flex profile, and fully in stock (Synth Sunset, Kabukicho, Neon Abyss, Neon Night). Second, two uniform KAM sets that finally nail palette and profile but are mid-restock (alphas kits only, right now).

Bucket 02

Pastel · Mintlodify · Cottagecore-Witch

Soft sage, matcha, lavender and butter-yellow; gentle gradients and botanical / celestial motifs. The deepest bucket — anchored by YuniKeyCo (the lavender you loved, now joined by its matcha, pastel-gradient and kawaii siblings) and by Mintlodica, whose “Pastel Dreams” gives the mintlodify look its name — plus matcha, elegant sage and greenhouse-botanical sets alongside.

Near miss — right vibe, wrong profile

Lush and on-theme, but Cherry profile.

Bucket 03

Ukiyo-e Heroes · Game-Art & Woodblock

Named for Jed Henry’s series, this bucket is about interesting artistic takes on the same IPs he reimagines — now spanning Zelda, Pokémon (Pikachu, Sylveon, a 151-key Pokédex), Mario & the Famicom, Animal Crossing, Kirby and Studio Ghibli (Totoro) — plus the woodblock that inspired them, from Hokusai’s Fugaku to a ukiyo-e Coral Sea wave. The art and the character are the point; flat / uniform profile and PBT throughout.

YuniKeyCo is the star of the pastel bucket — clean gradient-minimalist and soft-illustrative MOA/KOA sets (lavender, rainbow, sakura, ocean, matcha, plus the new gradient + kawaii siblings) — with Mintlodica (“Pastel Dreams”), Akko and KPrepublic rounding it out. For game-IP art, PopKeyCap (Mario, Famicom, Animal Crossing), joorkey (a 151-key Pokédex), Keysium (Totoro), XVX and GamaKay (ukiyo-e Fuji & waves) do the heavy lifting; NoisyCaps, AshborneCaps and AdventureKeycaps cover Zelda and Pokémon on Etsy. Cyberpunk is the hard one: true flat-profile bi-lighting is rare, so the best pink/purple/blue sets are Cherry-profile near-misses (Glorious Synth Sunset, Keysium Kabukicho) — and the two that get the profile right, Keyreative’s KAM 80s After Dark and Superuser (via DeskHero / DailyClack), are between production runs.
